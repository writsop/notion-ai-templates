## 🤖 AI Content Idea Generator

### Brainstorm Prompt
```
I'm a content creator in the [NICHE] space.

My content pillars are:
1. [Pillar 1]
2. [Pillar 2]
3. [Pillar 3]

My audience is: [describe target audience]

Recent trending topics in my niche: [list any you know]

Please generate:
1. 10 content ideas for this week (mix of platforms)
2. 3 "evergreen" content ideas that will stay relevant
3. 2 controversial/hot take ideas to drive engagement
4. 1 collaboration idea with suggested creator types

For each idea, include:
- Platform
- Format (reel, carousel, thread, video, etc.)
- Hook/title
- Key talking points (3-5 bullets)
- Best posting time
```

### Content Repurposing Prompt
```
I created this piece of content:
[Paste your content or describe it]

Original platform: [platform]
Performance: [views/likes/engagement]

Please help me repurpose this into:
1. Twitter/X thread (5-8 tweets)
2. Instagram carousel (8-10 slides, text for each)
3. YouTube Shorts/TikTok script (60 seconds)
4. Newsletter segment (300 words)
5. LinkedIn post (professional angle)
```

### Viral Hook Generator
```
Topic: [your topic]
Platform: [target platform]
Tone: [educational/entertaining/inspiring/controversial]

Generate 10 hooks that would stop the scroll:
- 5 question-based hooks
- 3 surprising stat/fact hooks
- 2 story-based hooks

For each hook, explain WHY it works psychologically.
```
